## Description

The global financial meltdown that took place in Fall 2008 caused millions of job 
and home losses and plunged the United States into a deep economic recession. 
Matt Damon narrates a documentary that provides a detailed examination of the 
elements that led to the collapse and identifies keys financial and political players. 
Director Charles Ferguson conducts a wide range of interviews and traces the story 
from the United States to China to Iceland to several other global financial hot spots. 

At the end of the film, we will discuss the similarities between the 2008 financial
crisis and the contemporary AI bubble.
